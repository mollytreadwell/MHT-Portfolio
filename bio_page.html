About



Molly Treadwell is an entrepreneur, a creator and a lover of all things design. She currently is a freelance social media manager and garden designer in the southern New Hampshire and Monadnock region.

When Molly is not promoting local businesses and beautifying various physical and digital environments, she can be found trail running, kayaking, painting, traveling with her fiance or playing with her cat.
